# Term and Auto-Renewal (Approved Standard, US, v1)

The initial term of this Agreement (the "Initial Term") shall begin on the Effective Date and continue for the period specified in the applicable Order Form. Following the Initial Term, this Agreement shall **not** automatically renew. Renewal shall occur only upon a renewal Order Form executed by both Parties prior to the expiration of the then-current term.

If the Parties' standard practice for a particular Order Form is to permit auto-renewal, the following terms shall apply (and must be expressly opted into in the Order Form):

1. Each renewal term shall be twelve (12) months unless otherwise specified.
2. Either Party may prevent renewal by providing written notice of non-renewal at least sixty (60) days before the end of the then-current term.
3. Renewal pricing shall not increase by more than the lesser of (a) five percent (5%) over the immediately preceding term's pricing, or (b) the percentage change in the Consumer Price Index (CPI-U, US City Average) for the same period.
4. The Customer's right to terminate for convenience under the Termination clause is preserved.

## Risk Policy Notes

- **Default = NO auto-renewal.** Renewal requires affirmative consent.
- If auto-renewal is permitted: 60-day non-renewal notice is the floor (longer notice = higher friction = higher risk).
- Cap on renewal price increases is mandatory when auto-renewal applies.
- Auto-renewal that exceeds 12-month renewal terms requires Legal sign-off.
- "Evergreen" terms (auto-renewing indefinitely with no opt-out window) are prohibited.
